DAP aur NPK mein kya fark hai? Kharif paddy ke liye kaunsa behtar?
9:47 AM ✓✓
Bahut achha sawaal! 🌾 Dono nitrogen-phosphorus fertilizers hain — lekin key difference hai:

DAP (Di-Ammonium Phosphate)
18% Nitrogen + 46% Phosphorus. Transplanting ya basal dose ke liye ideal — roots aur tillering strong karta hai.

NPK (e.g., 12-32-16)
Balanced N, P, K — potassium bhi milta hai. Grain filling stage mein zyada effective.

✅ Paddy ke liye recommendation:
Basal mein DAP — phir 25-30 DAS par Brand NPK 12-32-16 use karein. Yield 15-20% tak badh sakti hai.

Apne area ke soil type ke hisaab se agronomist se confirm karein.
